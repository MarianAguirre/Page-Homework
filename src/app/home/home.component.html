HIDALGO DEL PARRAL

Welcome to Hidalgo del Parral, Chihuahua

Discover the charm of Hidalgo del Parral, a historic city surrounded by hill and legends. Once a silver mining capital and the final chapter of Pancho Villa’s life, Parral invites you to explore its colonial streets, vibrant culture, and breathtaking natural landscapes. Whether you're visiting for history, adventure, or authentic Mexican flavor — you'll find it here.
