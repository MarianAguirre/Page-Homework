How to Get Here

By Air

The nearest airport is in Chihuahua City, with daily national and international flights. From the airport, you can rent a car or take a comfortable intercity bus to Parral (about 3.5 hours).

By Bus

Parral is well-connected by major bus lines from cities like Chihuahua, Durango, and Ju√°rez. Buses arrive at the Central de Autobuses, just a few minutes from downtown.

By Car

If you're driving, Parral is located along Highway 45 and Highway 24, making it easily accessible from northern and central Mexico. Roads are in good condition and the scenic views make the ride enjoyable.

Getting Around

Getting Around

Parral is compact and walkable, especially around the historic center.


You can also:

When to Visit

When to Visit


  • Best Weather: March to May and September to November (mild and dry)

  • Festival Season: July for Jornadas Villistas

  • Cool Winters: December and January can be chilly at night, but pleasant during the day
